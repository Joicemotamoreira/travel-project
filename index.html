Why Fortaleza?

Beautiful Fortaleza, the Land of the Sun!

Fortaleza, the vibrant capital of Ceará in northeastern Brazil, is a tropical paradise known for its stunning beaches, lively culture, and year-round sunshine. Visitors can relax on famous beaches like Praia do Futuro, explore the rich history at the Dragão do Mar Cultural Center, and enjoy Fortaleza's vibrant nightlife and culinary delights. Whether you're looking for sun-soaked relaxation or a taste of Brazilian culture, Fortaleza offers the perfect blend of natural beauty and urban excitement.

Places to visit

My favorite spots in Fortaleza

Shopping Iguatemi

Iguatemi Fortaleza is a large shopping mall in Ceará, featuring diverse stores, dining, and entertainment. Opened in 1982, it's a popular spot for shopping and leisure.

Address:

Av. Washington Soares, 85 - Edson Queiroz

What I like about it

I love this place because I've been coming here since I was a child. I really enjoy the atmosphere, the restaurants, the shops, and the cinema—everything is so pleasant. Plus, it's the perfect spot to escape the heat.

Beira Mar

Beira Mar in Fortaleza is a lively waterfront area with beautiful beaches, a busy boardwalk, and local markets. It's a popular spot for relaxation and experiencing local culture.

Address:

Av. Beira Mar, 3697 - Mucuripe

What I like about it

I love this area, especially in the late afternoon to watch the beautiful sunset, stroll through the craft market, and finish the day with delicious garlic shrimp, a local culinary specialty.

Explore

Barraca América do Sol

America do Sol is a popular beach bar in Fortaleza, offering great seafood and a relaxed atmosphere, perfect for enjoying a day by the ocean.

Address:

Av. Zezé Diogo, 4265 - Praia do Futuro

What I like about it

I love this beach bar! It’s ideal for enjoying the sea without traveling far. With excellent facilities for tourists, it also serves some of the best crab in the city.
